CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file must include the following exact column headers (case-sensitive):

  • SAP Upload Date – in YYYY-MM-DD format (required)
  • ISMS Upload Date – in YYYY-MM-DD format (optional)
  • Delivery Date – in YYYY-MM-DD format (optional)
  • Doc Type – e.g., "DR" (optional)
  • DR Number (optional)
  • Customer Name – Branch/customer name (required)
  • Qty – Quantity (required)
  • From Whse – From warehouse (optional)
  • To Whse – To warehouse (optional)
  • Model – Product model (optional)
  • Serial Number – Unique serial number (required)
  • ITR SO – ITR/SO number (optional)
  • DR IT – DR/IT reference (optional)
  • CBM – Cubic meters (required)

⚠️ Important: Use the downloadable template to avoid formatting errors.

✅ Duplicate Prevention: Records are validated against existing data using the combination of SAP Upload Date, Customer Name, and Serial Number. Duplicate rows will be automatically skipped during upload.

📊 Auto-Summary: Upon successful upload, LCL summary records will be automatically created or updated, aggregating quantity and CBM by SAP Upload Date and Customer Name.
